ChatEng - AI English Speaking Practice APP

ChatEng是一款提供使用者能輕鬆透過與AI對話鍛鍊英語口說的應用程式。 應用程式包括個人化學習計畫、情境關卡與可愛虛擬角色對話、成就排行榜，激發使用者學習動力。 透過這些功能，可以幫助使用者提升英語口語能力。

ChatEng is an application that allows users to easily practice their English speaking through conversations with AI. The application includes personalized learning plans, situational levels, conversations with cute virtual characters, and achievement rankings to stimulate users' learning motivation. Through these functions, users can help improve their English speaking skills.

Process

Problem

由於chapGPT的問世，使得知識取得變得非常容易，目前市面上的線上教育平台價格較為昂貴，而AI取代真人家教能使知識取得成本降低。

Due to the invention of chapGPT, it has become very easy to learning. The online education platforms on the market are more expensive, and AI replacing real teachers can reduce the cost of learning.

Competitive Analysis

分析市面上現有的AI語言學習APP，多為主題式練習，情境式練習更能模擬現場英語會話。不過目前大多在英語程度分級上不明確，且使用者主動學習動機意願不高，降低平台黏著度，另外語音辨識能力有限也是一個技術面需要解決的問題。

Most of the AI language learning apps on the market are themed exercises, the scenario-based exercises are more effective in simulating real-life English conversations. However, most of these apps currently lack clear English proficiency levels and have low motivation to use, resulting in reduced platform engagement. Additionally, limited speech recognition capabilities remain a technical challenge that needs to be addressed.

AI英語會話平台的市場接受度高，但仍有不能完全取代真人會話之處


透過競品分析和使用者訪談，我們完成AI英語會話平台的 SWOT 分析 ，發現大眾對於AI英語練習接受度是高的，「無壓力的學習環境」和「即時反饋」功能對於用戶而言是最大優勢，但仍然有不能完全取代真人對話的缺點，例如語音辨識能力有限，導致答非所問的狀況。而與真人對話能更靈活的處理各種語言和文化差異。

Through competitive product analysis and user interviews, we completed the SWOT analysis of the AI English conversation platform and found that the general public has a high acceptance of AI English practice, and the "stress-free learning environment" and "instant feedback" functions are the most important for users. Advantages, but there are still disadvantages that cannot completely replace real-person dialogue, such as limited speech recognition ability, resulting in unanswered questions. Dialogue with real people can deal with various language and cultural differences more flexibly.

Insight

1. 語音辨識和回應能力可能有限

經訪談有使用者提到語音辨識能力有限，將會降低AI機器人的回應精準程度，但目前提高語音辨識能力的技術尚須仰賴科技發展，在未來有望可以提升辨識力。


2. 主動學習動機意願不高

在競品分析時，發現大多數競品APP在主動學習動機意願上是較低的，因此目標是藉由增加獎勵機制，增加使用產品的誘因，提升平台黏著度。


3. 沒有英語程度分級

多數APP皆使用情境作為分級，而非語言程度，因此目標是加入前測小考功能，確認使用者英語能力，再給予程度適當的英語會話情境。

1. Voice recognition and response capabilities may be limited

After interviews, some users mentioned that the limited voice recognition capabilities will reduce the accuracy of the AI ​​robot's response. However, the current technology to improve voice recognition capabilities still relies on technological development, and it is expected to improve the recognition capabilities in the future.


2. Low motivation for active learning

During competitive product analysis, it was found that most competing APPs have low motivation for active learning. Therefore, the goal is to increase incentives to use products and improve platform stickiness by adding reward mechanisms.


3. There is no English proficiency rating

Most apps use situations as grading rather than language level, so the goal is to add a pre-test quiz function to confirm the user's English proficiency, and then provide appropriate English conversation situations.

Hi-Fi Prototype

與AI對話

我們的產品的主要賣點是為使用者提供一個與AI進行英語口語對話的機會，從而幫助他們提高口語表達能力。 這項功能解決了許多使用者學習英語時的主要痛點，即缺乏練習口語的機會。

Converse with AI

The main selling point of our product is to provide users with an opportunity to have a spoken English conversation with AI, thereby helping them improve their oral expression skills. This feature solves a major pain point for many users when learning English, which is the lack of opportunities to practice speaking.

前測小考功能

我們的應用提供前測小考功能，幫助使用者快速評估自己的英語水平，以便更好地調整學習計畫。

Pre-assessment Quiz

Our app provides a pre-assessment quiz function to help users quickly assess their English proficiency so that they can better adjust their study plans.

學習計畫

應用程式還設有學習計畫功能，系統會定時通知使用者繼續使用應用程式，鼓勵他們保持學習的動力，提高平台的使用者黏著度。

Learning Plan

The application also has a learning plan function. The system will regularly notify users to continue using the application, encourage them to maintain their motivation to learn, and improve user stickiness of the platform.

情境關卡

我們的應用程式設計了不同的情境關卡，讓使用者可以與可愛的虛擬角色進行對話。 使用者可以透過成功完成關卡來獲得積分，這項功能旨在提高使用者的學習動力。

Scenario Levels

Our app has designed different scenario levels so that users can have conversations with cute virtual characters. Users can earn points by successfully completing levels. This feature is designed to improve users' learning motivation.

個人成就

使用者可以查看他們的個人成就，也可以在排行榜上查看其他玩家的積分。 這有助於激勵用戶努力學習，追求更好的成績。

Personal Achievements

Users can view their personal achievements as well as other players' points on the leaderboard. This helps motivate users to study hard and pursue better results.

免費與付費升級方案

我們的應用程式提供了免費和付費升級選項，讓用戶可以根據自己的需求選擇不同的服務。 這些付費升級方案為平台提供了可持續的經濟效益。

Final Design

Product Successes

在SUS量表中獲得了78.75分的分數，證明了產品的易用性在「可接受」範圍。

It obtained a score of 78.75 on the SUS scale, proving that the product's ease of use is within the "acceptable" range.

What I Learned

1. 透過User-Center-Design的設計方式，能確保設計符合使用者需求
2. 透過Prototype測試可以幫助產品迭代，使功能更完善。
3. 前期的使用者研究將會影響產品設計策略。
4. 後續的產品優化需要更多的測試者參與測試並提供反饋。

1. Through the User-Center-Design design method, we can ensure that the design meets the needs of users
2. Prototype testing can help product iteration and improve functions.
3. Early user research will affect product design strategy.
4. Subsequent product optimization requires more testers to participate in testing and provide feedback.
